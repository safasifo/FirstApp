What is GIS?

GIS (Geographic Information System) is a technology that captures, stores, analyzes, and visualizes spatial data. It helps us understand patterns, relationships, and trends in geography.

How GIS Transforms Life

  • Helps in urban planning and smart cities
  • Improves navigation and transportation
  • Supports environmental protection and disaster management
  • Aids decision-making in business and governance

Real-World Impact

Field GIS Application
Healthcare Tracking disease outbreaks
Agriculture Precision farming for better crop yield
Disaster Response Mapping floods, earthquakes, and emergency planning
